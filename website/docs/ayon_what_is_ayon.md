# What is AYON? and Who is it for?

AYON is a Production and Pipeline management platform.
- AYON features for different persona:
  - producer: Manageing projects, users, Task planning and Assignments, tracking progress, reviewing, delivering, team colaboration and communication, reports.
  - Artist: quality of life, managing DCCs, publishing products and reviewables to AYON. tool distrubtion.
  - Pipeline Developers and TDs: Pipeline extensibility and customization, automations.
- Open Source: AYON is opensource you can customize, modify, contribute. FSL.
- Studio Size/criteria: AYON is robust, and scalable. it can fit from small teams to very large teams. Also, you can have small team and scale at some point of time and scale down back afterwards.
- Local, Remote, Hybrid: AYON fit different setups, whether the whole team is in one place or distibuted around the globe with single source of truth as the main studio site.
